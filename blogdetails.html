Best ways to Network and Meet Like Minded people.

111 startups Meetup @Mobisoft Infotech

We atended this meetup on "Panel of Funded-Bootstrapped-Accelerated Startups" and found it really thought provoking of how much difference it makes to a startup which is either Funded or Bootstrapped and Vikrant the Host did the best of asking the Panelists some questions which defined their journey and their experiences. To my surprise the panelist also included the Pioneer Members of Startups Club, Cofounder of Aadhar Api Ritesh and Cofounder of Bharti Robotics Debashish, the meetup was beautifully handeled and it was awesome to see so many differnt industries on one platform.

The other part of the discussion of Audience to Panelists Questions was a other way around to get to know more about their startups and how did they thought the idea would work and belived in themselves, the networking part helped us grab some really important information and people from the crowd which were ready to guide us we fellow teenagers to any extent. So at the end of the day its really important to get out of your comfortable office space to know the world around who knows if you get to see your future competiors there which eventually make you work harder and add unique feature to your idea and startup.
