Our Next Big Step with C^portal

Unit C2

Begining with making a smart webapplication platform for a Formal connection between the Private Schools or Colleges we never thought we were working on a Buisness idea until the day when the authority talked to us make the same platform which we made for class 9th to all those other senior classes including class 11th and 12th.. which we thought would be easy too but we had to cope up with the server charges and other costs.

Then we evenually approached the principal and talked to them about the costs, well this was my first time to talk in a serious way to someone that too for money :D the principal called our computer teacher and asked her about it and she said it may be a cost issue if the traffic to the website or the data increases and hence forth they offered us a 4 times amount for this which made us think of what on earth are they promoting 4x charges, to my surprise the Principal was thinking about a Buisness plan of her own which we didnt knew about until today, when the students told me about the revenue the School wascollecting from the students each 6 Months for the service C^portal provided. lol amazing right? so what are we doing next is making other networks with other schools including a Private Institution called Chate Classes which is the most marketed coaching centre according to me... Hope this goes well cheers.

The next furthur updates will be posted as well so stay tooned.
